Primary Risk
Emerging · Mid-April 2026

Cinco de Mayo Delays + Communication Gap
The first credible fundamental concern identified this month. The narrative is logical, relatable, and easy to spread; it does not require insider knowledge and can be repeated by any retail participant who has followed the Cinco timeline.

The risk is not the delay itself but the absence of proactive communication from the company. Silence on a missed timeline converts a manageable operational update into a trust issue. The longer this gap persists, the more durable the concern becomes.
"You generally don't go from imminent vote to months of delays" — CEO.ca, mid-April 2026
